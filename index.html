The principles of design allow us to improve the UX and make our product user friendly.
Early focus on users and tasks: this focuses on who is the end user, and from that study their behavior and their main characteristics.
Empirical Measurement: Here, the users interact with the prototypes while their reactions and performance are observed. Simulations are common in this phase, and he objective is to test which design works best the user.
Iterative Design: Finally, in this phase, after design and development all the system is tested trying to find common errors ad fix them through multiple iterations or cycles of design.
